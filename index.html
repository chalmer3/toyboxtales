Our Story

WonderNest was born from a simple belief: every child deserves toys that truly inspire, and every moment of play is a little adventure.

A shop built with love

Founded in 2022, WonderNest started as a small weekend market stall filled with handpicked toys and trading cards. Today, we've grown into an online haven for curious kids and passionate collectors alike.

Every product in our store is personally tested and curated by our small team of parents, educators, and lifelong toy enthusiasts. We believe in quality over quantity — things that last, teach, and bring genuine joy.

We're based in Cebu City, Philippines 🇵🇭 and proudly ship nationwide.
