Our Story

From a small kiosk to Cebu's most-loved toy shop — here's how PlayWorld came to be.

How We Started

PlayWorld Shop began in 2018 as a tiny kiosk inside SM City Cebu. Founder Juan dela Cruz, a lifelong toy collector and Pokémon card enthusiast, noticed that Cebuano families had nowhere local to find authentic collectibles and quality children's toys at fair prices.


Starting with just 50 card packs and 20 toys displayed on a folding table, the response from the community was overwhelming. Within three months, the kiosk had grown into a full stall — and within a year, PlayWorld had its first proper store.

Our Mission

We believe play is not a luxury — it's how children learn, grow, and form lifelong memories with their families. Our mission is to make quality, imaginative play accessible to every Filipino family.


Every product we carry is hand-selected for quality and authenticity. We refuse to stock counterfeit or low-quality items, because your children deserve the real thing. We're proud to be a trusted name in Cebu for over 6 years.
